Hi, I'm Nicole L. Turner. And I'm glad you found your way here.

I believe that the life you want is already waiting for you. What stands between you and that life is not a lack of resources, talent, or opportunity. It is the mental and emotional weight you have been carrying: the patterns you inherited, the beliefs that were handed to you before you were old enough to question them, and the habits of thinking that keep producing the same results no matter how hard you try to change.

I know this because I have lived it.

Over the course of my adult life, I kept chasing the next thing, the next opportunity, the next version of "better," and still felt like something was missing. What I eventually discovered was that the real work was not out there. It was in here. It was in the way I was thinking. Once I started doing that work, everything changed.

That is the foundation of Detox For Your Life®.

I carry that same philosophy into everything created under this brand: that real transformation happens from the inside out. Through books, a podcast, speaking engagements, and original content, I am here to do one thing well: give you something to think about, sit with, and act on.

Welcome to the Detox.

The Detox Life

Living from
the Inside Out

Living a Detox Life does not mean everything is perfect. It means you are committed to something most people avoid: looking inward.

What It Means

The Detox Life
in Practice

01
Choose Your Thoughts on Purpose
Instead of defaulting to old patterns, you learn to catch your thoughts, examine them, and decide with intention what you will allow to take up space in your mind.
02
Examine What You Believe
Every belief you carry was handed to you before you were old enough to evaluate it. The Detox Life asks you to look at those beliefs and decide which ones still serve you.
03
Take Radical Responsibility
Not self-blame. Radical responsibility means owning your emotional life without beating yourself up, and recognizing that you have more power over your outcomes than you think.
04
Live by Design, Not Default
Build habits of mind that produce the outcomes you actually want. Stop accepting whatever has been thrown at you and start making deliberate choices about how you show up.
